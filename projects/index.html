MineWar

MineWar is a Minecraft anarchy survival server network.
Minecraft IP: minewar.net 
Its inspired by the game Rust. We have lootdrops, realistic cars, guns, turrets and all that without mods. We also have custom crafting and later custom weapon skins for donators and supporters. We also have furniture, instruments and much more. All that works just with a Resourcepack wich will be automatically installed when you join the Server with your normal Minecraft client. Make clans and build a base with your friends wich you will need to defend with your weapons!
